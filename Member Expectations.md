# Member Expectations

* Must contribute positively
* Must have a willingness to learn
* Must have a willingness to teach/support others
* Must communicate with leadership on availability*
* Must respect other members, leadership, and mentors
* Must follow no-tolerance policy for all discrimination, including, but not limited to: homophobia, racism, sexism, transphobia, religious discrimination
* Must fill out the following forms: (1) RP Lab** (2) Emergency Medical Information** (3) FIRST Registration (4) Iron Riders Roster

Failure to follow these rules will result in a conversation with Leadership and/or Coach(es). In extreme cases, failure to follow these expectations will result in a suspension by the Coach(es).

<sub><sup>* No penalization for good faith efforts; we understand that this team  is time-consuming and will do our best to work with your schedule.</sup></sub>

<sub><sup>**These forms will be available in-person.</sup></sub>
